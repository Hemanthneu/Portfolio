MLOps - Credit card fraud detection

◾Engineered a machine learning model to detect fraudulent transactions, incorporating Apache Airflow for automating data loading and preprocessing, and utilizing RandomForestClassifier for model development.
◾Utilized MLFlow for model monitoring, DVC for data storage in GCP cloud buckets, and deployed through Docker images in Google Cloud's Artifact Registry for efficient hosting on Vertex AI.
◾Implemented a strategy for continuous improvement, including bi-monthly model retraining with Airflow DAGs to adapt to new fraud patterns, enhancing the reliability of credit card fraud mitigation.

DeepFake Detection using Mesonet

◾Utilized the MesoNet-4 architecture, incorporating inverted residual blocks and an attention mechanism, to train on a dataset of 150,000 high-resolution images, optimizing for deepfake detection.
◾Applied global unstructured pruning at a 20% sparsity, enhancing the model's efficiency while maintaining a high accuracy rate of 97% in identifying deepfake content.
